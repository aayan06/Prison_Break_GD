# Prison Break 🔒

A 2D boss fight game where you must escape by defeating a massive multi-segment worm boss.

🎮 **Play it here:** https://gd.games/games/55980a9d-61aa-4052-8175-0e83714f8f25

---

## About
Prison Break is a collaborative 2D boss fight game built in GDevelop. 
You play as a square character who must defeat a multi-segment worm boss 
by dashing into its head while dodging its attacks.

## How to Play
- **A / D** — move left and right
- **Space** — jump
- **Shift** — dash (damages the boss)
- **S** — dive
- Dash or dive into the worm's HEAD to deal damage
- Avoid the body, projectiles, and laser beams
- Bring the boss health to zero to win

## Features
- Multi-segment worm boss with multiple AI states (orbiting, charging, following, patrol)
- Multiple attack types — projectiles, laser beams, and charge attacks
- Player mechanics — dash, dive, double jump, wall bounce
- Invincibility frames for both player and boss
- Dynamic health bars
- Multiple scenes — main menu, boss arena, win screen
